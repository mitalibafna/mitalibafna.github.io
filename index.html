This text is enclosed in a box. I am a postdoc at CMU hosted by Prof. Aayush Jain and Prof. Pravesh Kothari. Before this, I was a graduate student in theoretical computer science at Harvard University where I was advised by Prof. Madhu Sudan. Even before, I did my undergrad in computer science from IIT Madras.
My research is focussed on complexity theory and algorithms, specifically the complexity of combinatorial optimization problems, sum of squares algorithms and high dimensional expanders.
